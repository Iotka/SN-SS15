Hello, Todo-Maker!

This site will show you the steps to make your own todo.

Step One

Move to "MakeTodo" and open it.

Step Two

Fill the name of your todo, the deadline of your todo,
a description and the curent state.

Step Three

Submit and magic happends. If you want to change the infos,
then click edit on the front page.
